# Sure Cure Physiotherapy and Rehab Clinic

Sure Cure Physiotherapy and Rehab Clinic is located in Pune, Maharashtra.

## About the Clinic

The clinic is led by Dr. Sujata, who completed her Master's in Physiotherapy from the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik, through M.A.R. College of Physiotherapy and Research, Pune, in 2014. She has been practicing for the last 15 years.

Her specialization includes Musculoskeletal Sciences and Sports Medicine.
