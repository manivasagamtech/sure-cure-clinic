About the clinic

Focused on precise diagnosis, hands-on treatment, and practical recovery plans.

The clinic is built around calm, evidence-informed physiotherapy that addresses pain, movement restriction, and recovery goals without making the experience feel clinical or cold. The approach is detailed, personal, and centered on what each patient needs to get back to daily life, work, exercise, or sport.

Dr. Sujata's background in musculoskeletal sciences and sports medicine supports care for both persistent pain conditions and active rehabilitation. The treatment approach blends mobility work, manual techniques, targeted strengthening, and modern therapy tools.
